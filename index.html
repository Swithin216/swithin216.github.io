About Me

Hi, my name is Swithin. And I'm a Web/Graphic designer based in New Zealand. I mainly specialise in front-end development. I'm a UCOL graduate and I specialise in designing and developing responsive websites with eye-catching graphics and user friendly interfaces and layouts.

Researching into latest frameworks and best practices has greatly improved my web designing skills and helped up speed up the development process. I have a keen interest for animation, as seen in this website, I'm forever diving deep into the realm of SVG creation and animation. I have also done a fair share of research and development on parallax based designs. If you haven’t noticed already, the left panel in the Skills Section of this website has a parallax effect activated by the movement of your cursor on the X-axis.

Photoshop and Illustrator are my most frequently used photo editing/graphic designing tools. From just doodling on Photoshop to creating striking graphics on Illustrator, my journey through the realm of Graphic design has taught me that a person’s skill is a never ending beam of self-improvement that shines bright.
